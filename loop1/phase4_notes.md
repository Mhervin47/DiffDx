Phase 4 exemplar priorities (based on Phase 2 failure patterns):

1. CVST/IIH in young women on OCPs with neurological symptoms
   - Demonstrate: OCP is a thrombotic risk factor
   - Demonstrate: postural worsening of headache is high-information
   
2. Travel-associated bacterial gastroenteritis vs IBD
   - Demonstrate: acute onset + travel + fever favors infection
   - Demonstrate: absence of bloody stool argues against UC specifically
   
3. Measles in pediatric fever+rash with vaccination gap
   - Demonstrate: MMR status changes the differential
   - Demonstrate: rash pattern + Koplik spots if asked
   
4. Decisive closure at high confidence
   - Demonstrate: when probability exceeds 0.65, commit and recommend action
   - Counteracts the "0.4 plateau" pattern
   
5. Patient question handling
   - Demonstrate: acknowledge, brief plain-language reassurance, continue
   - Position acknowledgment correctly in turn structure
